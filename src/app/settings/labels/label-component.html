Product labels

Labels let you group your products into meaningful categories, making it easier for customers to browse and discover what they’re looking for. Whether it’s seasonal items, special promotions, or curated themes, collections help you showcase your products in an organized way.

By creating and managing labels, you can:
  • 🛍️ Highlight featured or best-selling products.
  • 🎯 Target specific customer interests (e.g., “Summer Collection”, “New Arrivals”, “Festive Deals”).
  • 📂 Keep your store organized and visually appealing.
