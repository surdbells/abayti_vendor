Notifications

Customize your alerts - get only the updates you care about, exactly when you want them.

  • New Order Received
    Get alerted the moment a new order is placed, including items, total, and delivery method.
  • Order Cancelled
    Be notified when an order is cancelled (by you or the buyer), with reason and affected items.
  • Custom Order Placed
    Receive a notice when a customer submits a custom order request, including specs and due date.
  • Refund Request Initiated
    Get a prompt when a buyer requests a refund, with order ID and next steps required.
  • Product Listing Status Updated
    Know when a product is approved, rejected, or moved between draft/active/hidden.
  • Payment Scheduled
    Heads-up when a payout is scheduled, showing amount, date, and orders included.
  • Payment Completed
    Confirmation that your payout was sent, with transaction reference and breakdown.
  • New Review Received
    Alert when a buyer leaves a rating or review, including stars and comment preview.
  • Weekly Performance Summary
    A weekly snapshot of sales, orders, traffic, and returns.
  • Monthly Performance Summary
    A monthly report with trends, bestsellers, and improvement tips.
  • Low Engagement Alert [A.I]
    Notification when views or clicks drop below a set threshold, with suggestions to boost visibility.
